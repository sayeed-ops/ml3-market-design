RulesSystem guarantees

Fixed right cluster

Divider + Notifications + Cart is one shared partial, rendered identically on A / B / C. Cart shows a count badge; a bell dot marks unread. Account avatar is not here — it stays in the sidebar foot.

Search & filters ≠ header

Per-page search, filters and segmented status tabs live in a content toolbar directly under the bar, so the bar stays a constant height and never competes with page controls.

One height, sticky

All variants are 56px and stick to the top of the scroll area. Bar B’s tab strip sticks with it. The old floating .m-pageacts and per-page .m-head are retired.

Overflow, never wrap

≤2 primary CTAs sit inline; extras collapse into a ⋯ menu left of the divider. On narrow widths the detail identity truncates with an ellipsis before anything else gives.
